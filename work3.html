Cozy Homey

Cozy Homey is a charming little house nestled in the middle of nowhere, offering partial interactivity. You can experience Cozy Homey in both day mode and dark mode. It switches to day mode when you hover over the platform and transitions to night mode when your cursor is off the platform. Additionally, you have the option to pause the cloud animation. Furthermore, you can open and close the blinds, operate the door, and even emit steam from the chimney.
