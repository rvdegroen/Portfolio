Similar Villagers for ACNH

My concept is called 'Similar Villagers for ACNH.' It revolves around finding similarities among villagers based on their gender, personalities, and species. I decided to create this concept because I have never come across a website or application that displays similar villagers based on gender, personalities, and species. Whenever I play Animal Crossing: New Horizons, I often wish to encounter specific villagers with particular characteristics, and that's where my app comes into play. Additionally, I have included graphs to provide users with an overview of all the villagers and their data. I have used to ACNH API for this project. This was my first project with Vite and D3.js.
