Chinchilla Fighter

This was my first JavaScript project, and although I only had basic knowledge, I was determined to create something that filled me with excitement. So, I decided to develop a small game inspired by Pokémon, but featuring a Chinchilla. As a user, you can play against the computer, and your goal is to win using the resources at your disposal. I chose a Chinchilla as the theme because I have them as pets myself, and I dedicate a significant amount of time to their care and well-being and of course I love them very much. I made the illustrations myself using Figma.
