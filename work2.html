Drawmotion | Eric's Chat

I created this app for Eric, one of my teachers, who has a disability. This app is developed with the four exclusive design principles in mind: studying the situation, prioritizing identity, ignoring conventions, and adding nonsense. Eric utilizes both his computer and phone, which is why the app is responsive. Due to his disability, he encounters challenges in reaching the upper part of his phone and struggles to hold the mouse in. There are certain things and functions in the app that should help Eric. The app is in dutch since my teacher is dutch.
