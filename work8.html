Dish Exchange

Conceptually, this was supposed to be a phone app where users could sign up and add their own dishes to their accounts, with the intention of trading them with others. People would be able to match with each other's dishes, contact one another, and discuss the possibility of an in-person dish exchange. I worked on this project for only a few weeks, and since it was my first time working with Node.js. Express, EJS and MongoDB, I could only implement one feature, which was the original (school) assignment. The feature I developed allowed users to create, add, edit, and delete their own dishes from the database. I fully designed the entire concept using Figma
